What We're Building
A sprint-level developer productivity scoring tool that gives every developer a transparent, fair, and consistent score each sprint — based on the same frameworks that Google, Microsoft, Meta, Uber and Spotify use internally.

The Three Pillars It's Based On

DORA (Google) — "How fast and safely does code get to production?" — drives our delivery and quality metrics
SPACE (Microsoft Research) — "Productivity is multi-dimensional, never one number" — drives our weighted, balanced scoring approach
DX Core 4 (Meta, Uber, Spotify) — "Speed means nothing without AI adoption and developer experience" — drives our AI leverage tracking and contribution bonus


What We Measure

Delivery — story points completed vs committed, PRs merged, code reviews done
Quality — bugs post-sprint, rework, failed QA, test coverage
AI Leverage — how frequently and effectively developers use AI tools
Activity — commits and lines of code, kept at low weight intentionally to prevent gaming
Contribution Bonus — manager-approved extra work like POCs, refactoring and CI/CD improvements that never show up in story points today
